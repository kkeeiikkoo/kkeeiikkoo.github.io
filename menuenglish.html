Kaiten Sushi

The price of sushi is depanding on the color of plates. Take the sushi from the belt or take the order of sushi, you will get the plate. When you check the bill, the price of meal is decided by how many plates you have and what color are of plates.

If you want to take the order of sushi, you can write the number of sushi on the sheet, then give it the chef. if you need wasabi, you can also ask chef for wasabi.
